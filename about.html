About Us

Welcome To Cresent Yoga

We are inventors, makers and curators of the best yoga and meditation products available. We hand make yoga bolsters, meditation cushions, and other beautiful props in Burnaby, Canada where we fill them with natural materials and send them out the door to you.

Why Us?

Perhaps you've experienced it, the benefits of yoga and meditation are life changing. Our goal is to share this experience with as many people as possible and keep the benefits of yoga and meditation rolling in for life.
We believe that props are for everyone. If you have an advanced practice, a new practice a stiff practice or any other practice, we have a variety of products that will help inspire you and meet you exactly where you are.
